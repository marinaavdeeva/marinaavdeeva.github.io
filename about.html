Welcome to the paradise!!!

"Treating yourself with delicious home-made crepes will boost your mood to the heaven."

La Creperie is a new cafe located on the seashore of Baltic Sea in Svetlogorsk,Russia. We deliver sweet crepes filled with fresh fruits and exclusive toppings, hot drinks and juices in a relaxed and cozy atmosphere. If you do not see something you love on the menu, we are happy to let you create your own made-to-order crepe! Pick from our healthy choices, fresh fruits or simple sweet toppings for a taste that is all your own. You can stay and enjoy delicious crepes either at our cozy place or your order can be wrapped in a neat package and handed to you to go. We look forward to having the pleasure of serving you.
